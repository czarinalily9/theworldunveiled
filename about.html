Mission and Vision

Philosophy

Founded on July 12, 2023, our mission is to provide the Philippine citizens with authentic and factual information, enabling our society to make well-informed decisions in the crucial selection process of the national legislative and administrative body whilst also shedding light onto pressing issues of today.

Through this initiative, we commit to giving back the power to Philippine citizens by offering accurate, unbiased information at the edge of their fingertips, thus positively supporting the power of democracy.

Our vision is to inaugurate a community that values concrete evidence in pursuit of progressive change. Through this, the organization will prompt our community to maintain and hold equity, integrity, and transparency in high regard.
