We started three projects in one month!

The #PulsoNgPubliko: An Online Political Discourse is a distinctive and engaging monthly activity that seeks to capture, evaluate, and convey the diverse thoughts and viewpoints of various members within and beyond the organization. This initiative aims to shed light on critical societal issues, particularly those related to politics, by harnessing the power of video content and online platforms.

This project will allow us to address these following goals:

  • To provide an in-depth and multifaceted perspective of public sentiment, fostering public awareness and well-informed discussion and thereby contributing to a healthier and knowledgeable public discourse.
  • To promote accurate and well-researched information, encourage critical thinking, ensure that discussions are based on reliable facts, and help prevent the unintentional spread of false information and misunderstandings on specific topics.
  • To create a more civil and courteous online environment for political discussions, where people from all walks of life can engage in meaningful conversations without resorting to personal attacks or inflammatory language.

    • The October Essay / Write-Up Competition is a project which invites insightful and innovative minds to analyse a recent State of the Nation Address speech delivered by BongBong Marcos Jr., focusing on the issues that were overlooked.

    Participants will craft compelling essays that delve into the societal, economic, environmental, or political challenges that require attention and propose viable solutions. This competition aims to foster critical thinking, encourage problem-solving, and provide a platform for sharing constructive ideas.
