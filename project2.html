The October Essay Competition

The October Essay / Write-Up Competition is a project which invites insightful and innovative minds to analyse a recent State of the Nation Address speech delivered by BongBong Marcos Jr., focusing on the issues that were overlooked.

Participants will craft compelling essays that delve into the societal, economic, environmental, or political challenges that require attention and propose viable solutions.

This competition aims to foster critical thinking, encourage problem-solving, and provide a platform for sharing constructive ideas.
